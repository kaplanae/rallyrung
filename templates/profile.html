Tennis Rating Guide (NTRP)

  • 3.0 — Consistent on medium-paced shots, developing placement.
  • 3.5 — Improved stroke dependability, directional control, developing variety.
  • 4.0 — Dependable strokes, can hit with depth and variety, solid serve.

  • 5.5 — Highly advanced. Can vary strategies and styles, strong tournament player.
